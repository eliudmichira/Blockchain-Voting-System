Getting Started

System Requirements

  • A computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet access
  • A modern web browser (Chrome 88+, Firefox 85+, Edge 88+)
  • Your National ID card
  • Access to your registered mobile phone

Accessing the System

  1. Open your web browser
  2. Navigate to the IEBC voting portal: https://vote.iebc.go.ke
  3. The welcome screen will display information about the current election

Registration

Registration must be completed before the election day. Follow these steps to register:

  1. Click the "Register to Vote" button on the welcome screen
  2. Enter your National ID number
  3. Complete the verification process:
    • Enter the OTP (One-Time Password) sent to your registered mobile number
    • Answer the security questions related to your registration information
  4. Create a secure password for your voting account
    • Use at least 12 characters
    • Include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters
    • Avoid using easily guessable information like birthdays or names
  5. Review your registration details and confirm

Wallet Setup

The BBEMS uses blockchain technology to secure your vote. You'll need to set up a digital wallet:

  1. Click "Set Up Wallet" after completing registration
  2. If you already have MetaMask installed:
    • Click "Connect Existing Wallet"
    • Authorize the connection when prompted by MetaMask
  3. If you don't have MetaMask:
    • Click "Install MetaMask"
    • Follow the extension installation instructions
    • Create a new wallet following the MetaMask setup guide
    • Write down your recovery phrase and store it securely
    • Return to the voting portal and click "Connect Wallet"

Authentication

On election day, you'll need to authenticate your identity:

  1. Navigate to the IEBC voting portal
  2. Click "Login to Vote"
  3. Enter your National ID number and password
  4. Complete the two-factor authentication:
    • Enter the OTP sent to your registered mobile number
  5. Connect your MetaMask wallet when prompted
  6. The system will verify your eligibility to vote

Candidate Selection

After successful authentication, you'll see the ballot interface:

  1. The ballot will display all races you're eligible to vote in
  2. For each race:
    • View all candidates with their photos and party affiliations
    • Click on a candidate's name to view their profile and platform
  3. Take your time to review all candidates before making your selection

Casting Your Vote

To cast your vote:

  1. For each race, select your preferred candidate by clicking the circle next to their name
  2. Review your selections carefully on the summary page
  3. If you need to change any selection, click "Edit Selections"
  4. When ready to submit, click "Cast My Vote"
  5. Confirm your intention to vote
  6. You'll be prompted to sign the blockchain transaction with your wallet
    • Click "Sign" in the MetaMask popup
    • This doesn't cost any cryptocurrency - it's simply to authenticate your vote
  7. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed on the blockchain

Verification

After casting your vote, you can verify it was properly recorded:

  1. You'll receive a unique transaction receipt on screen
  2. Save or print this receipt for your records
  3. Click "Verify My Vote" to check that your vote was recorded correctly
  4. Enter your transaction ID from the receipt
  5. The system will display the votes recorded in that transaction without revealing your identity

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Problem: The system doesn't recognize my National ID
Solution: Verify you're entering the correct ID number. If the problem persists, contact the IEBC support center.

Problem: I didn't receive the OTP on my phone
Solution: Click "Resend OTP". If you still don't receive it, verify your registered phone number at your local IEBC office.

Problem: MetaMask isn't connecting to the voting portal
Solution: Ensure MetaMask is unlocked. Try refreshing the page. Check that you're using a supported browser.

Problem: My transaction is taking too long
Solution: Blockchain transactions can take several minutes. Please be patient. If it doesn't complete after 10 minutes, contact the on-site technical support.

Support Contacts

If you encounter any issues not addressed in this guide:

  • Call the IEBC Voter Helpline: 1-800-VOTE-HELP (1-800-868-3435)
  • Visit the technical support desk at your polling station
  • Email: voter.support@iebc.go.ke

Privacy and Security

The BBEMS ensures your vote remains secret while providing transparency and security:

  • Your identity is verified before voting but is then disconnected from your actual vote
  • The blockchain records only that you voted and your selected candidates without linking the two
  • The system uses advanced encryption to protect all data
  • You can verify your own vote but cannot access anyone else's vote
